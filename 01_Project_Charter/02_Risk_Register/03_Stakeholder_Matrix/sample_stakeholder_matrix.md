# Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder | Role | Influence Level | Interest Level | Communication Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Project Sponsor | Approves budget and direction | High | High | Weekly executive updates |
| Project Manager | Oversees project delivery | High | High | Daily coordination meetings |
| Development Team | Builds project deliverables | Medium | High | Agile sprint meetings |
| Marketing Team | Supports communication and launch | Medium | Medium | Bi-weekly status updates |
| Customers | End users of the project | Low | High | Feedback sessions and surveys |
